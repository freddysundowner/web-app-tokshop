Sell Live, Sell More

🎥

Live Streaming

Sellers tap one button to go live from their phone or computer. Viewers watch in HD, chat in real-time, and buy products right from the stream. Schedule shows in advance so followers get notified before it starts.

🔨

Live Auctions

Sellers auction products during their live shows. Buyers place bids, compete in real-time, and highest bidder wins when time runs out. Two formats: timed auctions with countdown, or sudden-death where last bidder wins.

💰

Buy Now & Offers

Sellers list products at fixed prices for instant purchase. Buyers can also make offers to negotiate - sellers accept, reject, or counter-offer. Plus a trending marketplace where buyers browse and shop anytime.

🎁

Giveaways & Engagement

Sellers run 5-minute giveaways during shows - viewers tap to enter, system picks a random winner automatically. Viewers can also tip sellers to show support. When a show ends, sellers can "raid" another live show and send all their viewers there.

Payments & Operations Built-In

💳

Stripe Payments + Commissions

Buyers pay securely with credit cards via Stripe. You set commission rates - one rate for everything, or different rates per category. Money goes to sellers automatically, minus your cut. You earn on every single sale.

📦

Shipping & Order Tracking

Shipping costs calculate automatically at checkout via USPS. Sellers print shipping labels right from the app - no copy-pasting addresses. Buyers track their orders in real-time and get automatic updates when packages ship.

⚖

Disputes & Refunds

If something goes wrong, buyers open a dispute. You review the case and decide the outcome. Process refunds with one click. Sellers and buyers can also cancel orders when needed. Everything is tracked in one place.

🎬

Video Proof & Receipts

Every auction ending is automatically recorded as a short video clip. Buyers get video proof showing exactly what they won and the final price. No more "I didn't bid on that" disputes - the video shows everything.

Powerful Dashboards for Everyone

💻

Admin Dashboard

Your command center to run the entire platform. Approve new sellers, manage categories, set commission rates, handle disputes, view platform-wide analytics, and control everything from one place. Works on desktop for serious business management.

💼

Seller Hub (Desktop)

Sellers get their own web dashboard - just like Whatnot's Seller Hub. They log in from their computer to view orders, print shipping labels in bulk, track payouts, see sales analytics, manage inventory, and run their business efficiently. No more doing everything from a phone.
